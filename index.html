About

I'm Fatema Tuz Zannat — a graphic designer who believes good design should be felt before it's read. I've worked with university clubs, startups, and local brands to turn ordinary announcements into things people actually stop for.

My process starts with the wall, not the screen — what does this need to do when it's seen for two seconds in a crowded feed or a busy street? Everything after that is editing: cutting until only the necessary thing is loud.
