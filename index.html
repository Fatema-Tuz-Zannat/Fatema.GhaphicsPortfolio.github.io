About

I'm Fatema Tuz Zannat — a graphic designer who believes good design should be felt before it's read. For the past five years I've worked with restaurants, music venues, startups, and local brands to turn ordinary announcements into things people actually stop for.

My process starts with the wall, not the screen — what does this need to do when it's seen for two seconds in a crowded feed or a busy street? Everything after that is editing: cutting until only the necessary thing is loud.

When I'm not designing, I'm collecting old gig posters and pretending I'll learn risograph printing properly one day.
